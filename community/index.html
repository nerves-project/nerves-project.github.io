Nerves ❤️ Community

Nerves strives to be an open and community driven project.
We love to hear how others are building with Nerves and want to highlight others contributions whenever we can.

Want updates?

Follow @NervesProject@fosstodon.org or subscribe to the Nerves Newsletter.

Feeling social?

Come nerd out at the monthly Nerves Online Meetup.

Need help?

Use the Nerves Forum to find previously answered questions or ask for help.

You can also post to the #nerves channel on the Elixir Discord or submit a new issue on GitHub.
